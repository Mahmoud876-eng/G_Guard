How SwarmSense Works

Inspired by nature's most efficient systems, our distributed network of sensors collaborates to identify and verify environmental threats.

1

Deploy Micro-Agents

Distribute low-cost Raspberry Pi Pico nodes equipped with environmental sensors across your facility or area of interest.

2

Collective Monitoring

Each node continuously monitors for pollutants, toxic gases, temperature anomalies, and other environmental hazards.

3

Distributed Intelligence

Nodes share data wirelessly and use lightweight AI models to collaboratively identify and verify potential threats.

4

Early Warning System

Once a hazard is verified by the swarm, immediate alerts are sent to enable proactive response before damage occurs.
